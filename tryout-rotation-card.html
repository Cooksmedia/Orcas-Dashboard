Station Guide
S1
Serve Receive Coach or feeder attacks serves from the endline. Player passes to target zone. Score 1–5 on platform control, footwork, and target accuracy.
S2
Zone Serving Player serves 5 balls to called zones (1–6). Score 1–5 on consistency, pace, and placement. Aces = 5, net/out = 1.
S3
Setting Accuracy Tosses or passed balls fed to setter position. Player sets to zone 4 and zone 2 alternating. Score on height, location, and consistency.
S5
Read & React Digging Coach or hitter attacks at varying angles. Player reads, moves, and digs. Score 1–5 on read speed, platform angle, and control.
