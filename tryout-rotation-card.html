ONE COURT — STATION ZONES
S1 — Serve ReceiveBack half court. Feeder/coach tosses or serves from 10ft line. Passer targets zone 3.
S2 — Zone ServingFar endline. Players serve to called zones. Others shag and return balls.
S3 — Setting AccuracyCenter court. Ball tossed to setter position. Sets to zone 4 and zone 2 targets (cones).
S5 — Read & React DiggingFront half court. Coach or partner tips/hits from the net. Player reads and digs.
⚠️ Groups A + E share a station each round — 5 groups, 4 stations. Whichever station A and E share, split them to opposite sides of that zone and score them separately. Takes about the same time since groups D and E only have 2 players each.
Scoring Guide — 1 to 5 per player per station
S1
Serve ReceivePlatform control, footwork, passes to target. 5=perfect platform every time, 1=ball not playable.
S2
Zone Serving5 serves to called zones. 5=ace or perfect placement, 3=in but off-zone, 1=net or out.
S3
Setting AccuracyHeight, location, consistency. 5=hittable every time to both zones, 1=un-hittable.
S5
Read & React DiggingRead speed, platform angle, control. 5=digs cleanly to setter every time, 1=can't dig it.
