📖 What The Numbers Mean — Coach's Guide
👤
Active Users
People who visited the hub in the selected time period. Check this after sharing the hub link to see how many parents/players clicked it.
Good benchmark: 20+ users in the 24hrs after you share the link
📄
Top Pages
Which pages get the most visits. If the Skill Guides are at the top, players are self-studying. If only the home page shows, they're not going deeper.
Goal: see skill guide pages in the top 5
⏱️
Avg Engagement Time
How long people spend on each page. Under 10 seconds = they bounced. Over 60 seconds = they're actually reading and using it.
Skill guides should show 1–3 min average
🔄
Sessions
One session = one visit. A player who visits Monday and again Wednesday counts as 2 sessions. More sessions = players coming back regularly.
Returning users means the hub is valuable
📱
Device Type
Mobile vs desktop. Expect 80%+ mobile — 7th–9th graders use phones. If mobile % is low, parents (on laptops) are the main users.
High mobile = players are using it directly
📍
Location
Should show Phoenix / Chandler / Mesa / Gilbert area. If you see traffic from unexpected locations, someone shared the link publicly.
All traffic should be East Valley AZ
💡 How To Use Analytics As A Coach
5 Things to Check Weekly
1
After sharing the hub link — open Realtime Overview within 30 minutes. You'll see exactly how many people clicked your link and which page they landed on.
2
Check Pages & Screens weekly — if players are only visiting the home page and never going to Skill Guides or the Perimeter Defense page, you know you need to point them there directly at practice.
3
Spike after game day — check if traffic spikes after each Saturday game. Players who check the Game Schedule or Game Film right after a game are engaged. Players who don't visit at all after a game need a reminder.
4
Before tournament — open Pages & Screens and check if the Perimeter Defense page has visits in the 48 hours before game day. That tells you who is actually studying the system.
5
Month-over-month — set the date range to "Last 30 days" and compare to the previous month. Growing visit counts = the hub is becoming a habit. Flat or declining = time to re-promote it to the team.
