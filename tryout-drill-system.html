How to Run Your Tryout
01
Build Your Roster
Add every tryout athlete in the Roster tab. Name, jersey number, grade, and position they're trying out for.
02
Run the 7 Stations
Rotate players through each drill. Every drill has a clear 1–5 rubric so any coach or assistant can score consistently.
03
Score in Real Time
At the bottom of each station, tap 1–5 for each player as they go. Add quick notes right then — don't wait until later.
04
Review Results
Go to Results for a ranked leaderboard, letter grades, and individual breakdowns per player. Sort by any drill or overall.
